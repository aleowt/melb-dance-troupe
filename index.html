About

Melbourne Dance Troupe is a hip hop dance troupe based in Melbourne and has performed mainly in small theatres and dance festivals around Melbourne and a few large provincial towns in Victoria, including Geelong, Ballarat and Bendigo. Hip hop is a cultural and art movement originated from New York City, predominantly created by African Americans.

Aims & Objectives

Melbourne Dance Troupe aims to spread the influence of hip hop in order to:

  • Promote unity amongst the youth
  • Bring awareness to social issues
  • Increase diversity within the hip hop community

These are their goals:

  • Make it to regionals
  • Make it to nationals
  • To dance alongside professionals

As hip hop is a movement born of dark times, it was something that gave hope to the people. Melbourne Dance Troupe aims to spread this positivity as much as they can.

Works

Show'em is a piece that showcases women's skills in hip hop. It's a feature dance by two of the members. The synchronisation and dynamic moves demonstrate power in teamwork and the endless possibilities of working together.
